About Us

The Traffic Management uses cutting-edge technology to ensure smoother traffic flow . By integrating sensors, cameras, and real-time analytics, we aim to reduce traffic congestion and prioritize emergency vehicles like ambulances.

Our system combines machine learning, computer vision, and IoT technologies to detect ambulances, optimize traffic light cycles, and improve urban mobility, ensuring quick emergency access and efficient city management.

Traffic Management System

Our Traffic Management System leverages real-time data collection to optimize traffic flow and reduce congestion in urban areas. This system uses sensors and cameras to monitor traffic conditions and dynamically adjust traffic light cycles based on traffic volume.
